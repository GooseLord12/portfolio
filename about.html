About Me

Hi, I'm Braden.

Designer, marketer, and the kind of person who genuinely gets excited when a button hover animation feels just right. I'm currently finishing up my undergrad degree in Interactive Media - Web Design at the University of Central Florida and will be returining to UCF pursue my Master's degree in Business Administration (I just love school so much!) With a background in graphic design and customer service after working for the Department of Housing and Residence Life at UCF for the past four years, I have honed my skills and worked on a variety of personal and professional projects from website development and prototyping to marketing for large scale events for whole student communities. Long story short, I think I have mad skills in both design and marketing.

I live somewhere between "make it beautiful" and "make it functional" -- and honestly, I think the best work happens when you stop treating those as opposites. I love building things that look incredible and actually do something meaningful.

When I'm not obsessing over layouts or campaign metrics, you'll probably find me discovering some obscure design tool, debating whether that shade is coral or salmon, or coming up with project ideas at 2 AM that I'll definitely start and maybe finish. I love playing video games (don't judge my skills), I practically live at the movie theater, I also have a part-time residence at the gym, and between drawing and reading comics, I'm usually spending time with family and friends. Click through to see some of the projects I've worked on and reach out if you have an idea that I can help make a reality no matter how zany it is.
